
Golden State Warriors' Kevin Durant leads star-studded Team USA's challenge as they open their Olympic basketball campaign against China



The American men's basketball team begin their quest for a 15th Olympic gold when they open their campaign against China on Saturday.Despite the absence of LeBron James and Steph Curry, the American team is once again packed with some of the NBA's biggest names, including Draymond Green, Klay Thompson, Carmelo Anthony and Kyrie Irving.But there is no doubting the leader of the team, standing 6ft 9in with size 15-and-a-half feet and an arm length of more than 40 inches - Golden State Warriors star Kevin Durant.
