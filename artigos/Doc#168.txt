
Rio Olympics day one highlights: Adam Peaty sets new world record, Team GB's women beat Australia and rugby sevens makes its Games debut



Adam Peaty made a phenomenal start to the Rio Olympic Games as the British swimmer posted a new world record in the 100m breaststroke heats.The Team GB star bettered his own record by clocking a time of 57.55secs before later booking his place in the final.There was disappointment for Chris Froome and Geraint Thomas in the men's road race, with the Tour de France winner never putting himself in contention and Thomas falling on the final descent when well set for a medal.Great Britain's women's hockey and rugby sevens teams both enjoyed winning starts, however, on a hectic day of sport.
